<p><p>The semi-metallic gasket sheet market is divided into two primary types based on flange design: flat flange and non-planar flange. Flat flange gaskets are used in standard applications where surfaces are parallel, allowing for even compression and sealing. Conversely, non-planar flange gaskets accommodate uneven or irregular surfaces, ensuring a tight seal despite variations in geometry. Both types are critical in industries requiring reliable sealing solutions under high pressure and temperature, offering durability and enhanced performance.</p></p>

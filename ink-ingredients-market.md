<p><p>The ink ingredients market comprises various types based on their solvent composition. Solvent-based inks use organic solvents for quick drying and high adhesion, often used in industrial applications. Water-based inks primarily utilize water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly, suitable for packaging and printing. Oil-based inks, traditionally used in high-quality print applications, provide vibrant colors but may have higher VOC emissions. UV-based inks cure rapidly under ultraviolet light, offering durability and vibrant colors for various substrates, ideal for specialized printing tasks.</p></p>

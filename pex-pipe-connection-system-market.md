<p><p>The PEX pipe connection system includes two primary market types: brass and plastic. Brass connections are favored for their durability and resistance to high pressures, making them suitable for both residential and commercial applications. They provide a secure fit and are less prone to leaks over time. In contrast, plastic connections, often made from materials like polybutylene, offer lightweight, flexible options that are resistant to corrosion. They are typically easier to install and are popular for residential plumbing systems, reducing overall installation costs.</p></p>

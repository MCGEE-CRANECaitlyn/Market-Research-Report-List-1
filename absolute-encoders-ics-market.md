<p><p>The absolute encoders integrated circuits (ICs) market is primarily divided into single-turn and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide a unique position value within a single complete rotation, making them ideal for applications requiring precise rotational measurement in a limited range. In contrast, multi-turn encoders can track multiple revolutions, offering absolute position data over extended rotations. This capability is critical in applications where maintaining positional accuracy over time is essential, such as robotics and industrial automation.</p></p>

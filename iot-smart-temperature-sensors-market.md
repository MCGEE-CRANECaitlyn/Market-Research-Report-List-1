<p><strong>The IoT Smart Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Sensing</li><li>Non-Contact Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT smart temperature sensors market is segmented into two main types: contact sensing and non-contact sensing. Contact sensing sensors measure temperature by directly touching the object, providing precise readings suitable for applications like industrial processes and food safety. In contrast, non-contact sensing utilizes infrared technology to gauge temperature from a distance, making it ideal for scenarios where direct contact is impractical, such as monitoring equipment or assessing body temperatures in healthcare settings. Both types enhance data collection and efficiency across various industries.</p></p>

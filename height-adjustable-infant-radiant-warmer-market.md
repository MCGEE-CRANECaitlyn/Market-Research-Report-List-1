<p><p>The height-adjustable infant radiant warmer market comprises two main types: manual control and automatic control units. Manual control warmers require healthcare professionals to adjust temperature and height settings manually, providing flexibility but potentially increasing the risk of human error. In contrast, automatic control warmers utilize advanced sensors and technology to maintain optimal thermal regulation and adjust height autonomously, ensuring consistent warmth and minimizing caregiver workload. Both types are crucial for providing appropriate care to premature and critically ill newborns.</p></p>

<p><p>The commercial vehicle brake fluids market consists of various types, primarily categorized as DOT 3, DOT 4, and DOT 5. DOT 3 is glycol-based, suitable for light vehicles, offering moderate performance with a boiling point of 205°C. DOT 4 also uses glycol but has a higher boiling point of 230°C, making it appropriate for heavier vehicles. DOT 5 is silicone-based and non-hygroscopic, providing stability in extreme temperatures, and is used in specialized applications such as racing or classic cars.</p></p>
